Faith in Jesus Christ and His Atonement

The reason for the hope that is in the people with faith in Jesus Christ convinces many people to have faith in him. You may not personally have experienced that but here is what they have faith in.

  1. One day be able to live with their families in an eternity
  2. Be forgiven for the sins they have commited
  3. Become free to choose for themselves to follow him
