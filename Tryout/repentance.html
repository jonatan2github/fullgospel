Repentance through the Atonement of Jesus Christ

When life gets you down it is so easy to say that it is everyone else's fault. Members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter- Day Saints tend to want to take responsibility for that. They know changing their way of thinking, dealing with other people is what they came here to Earth for! Their Prophet recently said:

"When Jesus asks you and me to “repent,” He is inviting us to change our mind, our knowledge, our spirit, even the way we breathe. He is asking us to change the way we love, think, serve, spend our time, treat our wives, teach our children, and even care for our bodies."

Nothing is more liberating, more ennobling, or more crucial to our individual progression than is a regular, daily focus on repentance.
